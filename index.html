About

I am a PhD Candidate in Engineering Science at Simon Fraser University, BC, Canada, specializing in robotics, control systems, and artificial intelligence. My research focuses on integrating UAVs with manipulators for aerial manipulation, utilizing vision-based systems, dynamic modeling, machine learning, and advanced control design to solve real-world challenges.

I have extensive experience in AI and machine learning, applying techniques like deep neural networks (FNN, TCN, RNN) for dynamic modeling and reinforcement learning (RL) for control optimization. Additionally, I am exploring the potential of large language models (LLMs) to improve intelligent human-system interactions. My goal is to create adaptive, AI-driven solutions that tackle significant challenges and positively impact lives worldwide. I am seeking an opportunity to apply my expertise in AI research, engineering, and development in a challenging role that fosters professional growth, offers valuable experiences, and supports personal development.
